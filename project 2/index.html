About TEDxDYP

TEDxDYP is an independently organized TED event licensed by TED. We bring together remarkable speakers and engaged audiences to spark meaningful conversations and inspire action.

Our Mission

To spread ideas that inspire innovation and positive change in our community.

Our Community

A diverse group of students, faculty, and thought leaders united by curiosity.

Our Values

Authenticity, inclusivity, and the power of ideas to transform lives.

Our Impact

Creating lasting change through powerful storytelling and meaningful connections.
